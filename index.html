FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
Common satellite radio questions answered clearly
QHow do I activate satellite radio in a new car?
You will need your Radio ID (RID/ESN) from the radio display or Settings menu. Call our advisor line and we will walk you through every step.
QWhy does my signal keep dropping?
Signal interruptions are often caused by tunnels, tall buildings, or dense trees. If it persists in open areas, a remote refresh may be needed — our advisors explain how to request one.
QHow do I cancel or pause my subscription?
Have your account number ready and call our line. We explain each step clearly so you can make the right decision.
QI was charged after cancelling — what do I do?
Our advisors walk you through how to review your account status and the steps to dispute unexpected charges with your provider.
QCan I transfer my subscription to a new vehicle?
Yes. You will need the new vehicle's Radio ID and your account details. Our team explains the transfer process step by step.
QHow can I find out about renewal deals?
Existing listeners are often eligible for promotional rates. Our advisors can guide you on what to ask your provider at renewal time.
QThe satellite radio app is not working.
Try force-closing and reopening the app. If the issue continues, call us and we will guide you through the next steps.
QWhat channels come with each plan?
Channel availability varies by plan tier. Our advisors can explain the differences so you can choose what fits your preferences.
